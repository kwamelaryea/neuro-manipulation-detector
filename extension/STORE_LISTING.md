## Detailed description

ZDrive Neuro Lens scans webpage text and scores it for neural manipulation — the kind engineered into headlines, social feeds, and marketing copy. You get a 0-10 Manipulation Index showing exactly where emotional pressure is designed in.

**What you get**

- Real-time fast scan (~2s): Manipulation Index as you scroll. See emotional pull vs. rational guard activation.
- Deep neural scan (~1-3 min): TRIBE v2 brain-encoding analysis on dedicated GPU. Pinpoints which neural regions of persuasive architecture are most active.
- Brain signal breakdown: Visual map of limbic (emotional) and prefrontal (rational) engagement — what's being triggered, not just that you're being triggered.
- Score badge on every page: color-coded manipulation indicator at a glance.

**How it works**

Every page gets scored automatically on scroll. Most content lands between 0-4 (neutral/editorial). Tabloid headlines, fear-based content, and aggressive marketing typically score 6-9. When manipulation is detected, Neuro Lens shows the breakdown: which emotional buttons are being pressed, how rational filters are being bypassed.

The scoring framework is based on Meta FAIR's TRIBE v2 brain-encoding model, which predicts cortical activation patterns from text stimuli. Fast scans use LLM reasoning over this framework. Deep scans run actual neural inference on A10G GPU hardware.

**Who it's for**

- News readers who want to know if they're being influenced before they share
- Researchers and journalists fact-checking emotional framing in coverage
- Anyone curious why content feels designed to make them angry, afraid, or urgent

**Privacy**

The extension reads visible page text to analyze it. Text is sent to our backend for scoring — no personal data, browsing history, or cookies are stored. All data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS). See the full privacy policy for details.
